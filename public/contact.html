Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get help with medicine requests?

Emergency requests are processed within 24 hours. Regular requests typically take 2-3 business days. We prioritize based on medical urgency and availability.

Is there a cost for MedReach services?

Our basic services are free for those in need. Some specialized services may have minimal fees, but we work with everyone regardless of ability to pay.

How can I become a volunteer?

Visit our Volunteer page to learn about opportunities and fill out an application. We provide training and support for all volunteers.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the greater metropolitan area and surrounding counties. Our mobile units extend our reach to rural communities.

How do you ensure medication safety?

All medications are sourced from licensed pharmacies and healthcare providers. Our medical professionals verify prescriptions and provide safety guidance.

Can I donate medications?

We follow strict FDA guidelines for medication donations. Please contact us to learn about acceptable donations and our verification process.
