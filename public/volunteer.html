Why Volunteer with MedReach?

Directly help community members access life-saving medications and healthcare resources.

Volunteer Opportunities

Medicine Delivery

Help deliver essential medications to community members who cannot travel to pickup locations.

  • Flexible delivery schedules
  • Local routes available
  • Vehicle provided if needed
  • Safety training included
2-4 hours/week

Support Hotline

Provide telephone support and guidance to community members seeking medical assistance.

  • Remote work available
  • Comprehensive training provided
  • Flexible shift scheduling
  • Mental health support
3-6 hours/week

Administrative Support

Help with data entry, inventory management, and coordinating volunteer activities.

  • Office or remote work
  • Computer skills helpful
  • Organized work environment
  • Professional development
4-8 hours/week

Medical Professional

Licensed healthcare providers offering consultation and medical guidance services.

  • Telemedicine consultations
  • Prescription reviews
  • Medical emergency support
  • Continuing education credits
2-6 hours/week

Community Outreach

Spread awareness about MedReach services and engage with local communities.

  • Event coordination
  • Social media management
  • Partnership development
  • Public speaking opportunities
3-5 hours/week

Technical Support

Help maintain and improve our technology platforms and digital infrastructure.

  • Website maintenance
  • Database management
  • App development
  • IT troubleshooting
4-10 hours/week
